How to Create Questions

1

2

Write Question: Enter your question text clearly

3

Add 4 Options: Provide exactly 4 multiple choice options

4

5

Question Writing Best Practices

Clear Language

Use simple, clear language that students can easily understand.

One Correct Answer

Ensure only one option is clearly the best answer.

Realistic Options

Make wrong answers plausible but clearly incorrect.

Check Grammar

Proofread questions for spelling and grammar errors.

Appropriate Length

Keep questions concise but comprehensive.

Fair Marking

Assign marks based on question difficulty.
